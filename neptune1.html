Neptune1 at a glance

Neptune1 is a practical water treatment and cost optimisation platform designed for industrial settings. It combines water balance analysis, treatment selection, cost modelling and value creation metrics.

Country & Region analysis

Water stress maps, discharge limits, and regional contexts pulled together with the operational decision model.

Company data & flow diagrams

Plant-level water flows, contaminant tracking and Sankey-style analysis for industrial users.

Treatment & cost estimation

Technology selection, CAPEX/OPEX calculation, and economic value insights for recycle and reuse strategies.

Neptune1 is part of AMC's applied platform suite. Use the `launch_neptune.bat` script in the Neptune1 folder or run `streamlit run Home.py` to explore the full interactive application.
