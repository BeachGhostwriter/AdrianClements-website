What the current app already does

  • Maps site context and regulatory limits
  • Models water balance and contaminants
  • Suggests treatment trains and checks compliance
  • Shows value creation, CapEx / OpEx and EBITDA impact

A platform for design, evaluation and decision support

Integrated workflow

Instead of separate tools, the app links plant context, engineering design and economics into one flow.

Scenario-based design

Users can compare recycle targets, treatment options and cost cases to see the impact on water savings and sustainability.

Business value lens
