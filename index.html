WAVE-inspired software for industrial water treatment design

The Neptune application already supports site assessment, treatment design, economics and value creation. This page adapts the DuPont WAVE approach into a professional product experience for the same capability.

Explore the application View conversion roadmap

What the current app already does

  • Maps site context and regulatory limits
  • Models water balance and contaminants
  • Suggests treatment trains and checks compliance
  • Shows value creation, CapEx / OpEx and EBITDA impact

A platform for design, evaluation and decision support

Integrated workflow

Instead of separate tools, the app links plant context, engineering design and economics into one flow.

Scenario-based design

Users can compare recycle targets, treatment options and cost cases to see the impact on water savings and sustainability.

Business value lens

The current app already shows EBITDA bridge, simple payback, savings and investment trade-offs.

How to make it feel more like WAVE

DuPont WAVE is successful because it offers a guided, single-interface design experience with clear value propositions and strong product storytelling.

Guided wizardOne interfaceScenario comparisonDownloadable reports

Suggested WAVE-style conversion roadmap

1. Reframe the appCreate a stronger product story around design, evaluation and optimization.
2. Build a guided workflowMove from setup to water balance to treatment selection and economics in one journey.
3. Add WAVE-like modulesIntroduce a technology library, design assistant and scenario comparison panel.
4. Elevate reportingProvide downloadable reports and investment cases that look like commercial software output.

How this page can be used

This landing page is designed as the public-facing entry point for a broader Neptune/WAVE-style offering. It can sit on your site as a product story, while the Streamlit application remains the underlying engine for analysis and modelling.

Neptune

Core
